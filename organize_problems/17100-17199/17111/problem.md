## 문제

Is it rated?

## 입력

You will be given the name of the past contest held at Codeforces within a single line.

## 출력

Print 1 if the contest was rated, 0 else.

Semi-rated contest(s?) like [https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/56101](./001_56101) will be considered as a rated contest.
