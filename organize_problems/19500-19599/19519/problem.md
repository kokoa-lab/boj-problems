## 문제

There are $N$ circles on a plane. The center of the $i$-th circle is $(x\_i, y\_i)$, and the radius of this circle is $\sqrt{x\_i^2 + y\_i^2}$.

Count the number of lattice points (points with integer coordinates) that are inside at least one of the circles. Note that the boundaries are considered to be inside.

## 입력

$N$  
$x\_1$ $y\_1$  
$x\_2$ $y\_2$  
$\vdots$  
$x\_N$ $y\_N$

## 출력

Print the answer in a single line.
