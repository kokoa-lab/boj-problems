## 문제

Alex has four sticks with positive integer lengths $a\_1$, $a\_2$, $a\_3$, and $a\_4$ ($a\_1 \le a\_2 \le a\_3 \le a\_4$).

She wants to place them on a plane in such a way that each stick is parallel to one of the two coordinate axes, and the area enclosed by these sticks is as large as possible.

Find this maximum enclosed area.

## 입력

The input contains four positive integers $a\_1$, $a\_2$, $a\_3$, and $a\_4$, each on a separate line, denoting the lengths of the sticks in non-decreasing order ($1 \le a\_1 \le a\_2 \le a\_3 \le a\_4 \le 100$).

## 출력

Print the maximum area that can be enclosed.
