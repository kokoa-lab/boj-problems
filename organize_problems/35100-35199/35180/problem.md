## 문제

The North Sea is littered with $N$ pieces of garbage, numbered $1 \dots N$. The $i$'th piece is at position $(x\_i, y\_i)$ and has a weight of $w\_i$. As part of a cleanup effort, all garbage within a rectangular area will be collected. This area has a width of $W$ and a height of $H$, but its location has not yet been decided.

Determine the maximum total weight of garbage that will be collected if the cleanup area is positioned optimally.

## 입력

The first line contains the integers $N$, $W$ and $H$.

The $i$'th of the next $N$ lines contains the integers $x\_i$, $y\_i$ and $w\_i$.

## 출력

Print an integer: the maximum total weight of garbage that can be collected.
