## 문제

Obi-Wan has just sent an urgent message to the Jedi Temple, but the message has been garbled by subspace interference. All of the strings “th” have been replace with “iiing”. Your job is to unscramble the message.

## 입력

One or more lines of garbled text.

## 출력

The ungarbled text.
